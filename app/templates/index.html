About Guessture

PDFile is a free tool created to help students understand text better by providing media content and also provide keypoints in the text.

This tool extracts the contents from each chapter and provides the relevant multimedia content like video tutorials from YouTube™.

PDFile also summarizes the chapter to help students revise before the exams.
