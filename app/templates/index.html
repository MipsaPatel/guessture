About Guessture

Guessture is a deep learning application that helps the speech and hearing impaired converse better by converting sign language to text.

This application takes videos of sign language gestures and identifes the action represented by the video.

The action can be mapped to a letter, phrase or sentence, thus converting the sign language gesture to text.
